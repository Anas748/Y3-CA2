Monster Mayhem

Game Rules

  • Each round, the player with the fewest monsters on the grid goes first. If tied, the first player is chosen randomly.
  • On a player's turn, they can place a vampire, werewolf, or ghost on their edge of the grid. They cannot move this monster on the same turn.
  • Monsters can move any number of squares horizontally or vertically, or up to two squares diagonally. They can move over their own monsters but not over other players' monsters.
  • If two monsters end on the same square:
    • Vampire vs. Werewolf: Werewolf is removed.
    • Werewolf vs. Ghost: Ghost is removed.
    • Ghost vs. Vampire: Vampire is removed.
    • Same type: Both are removed.
  • A player's turn ends when they decide or if they have no monsters left to move.
  • A round ends once all players have had a turn.
  • A player is eliminated after losing 10 monsters. The last player remaining wins.
